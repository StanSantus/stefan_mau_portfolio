My Way into Data

My love for data was always there, even though I did not know about it. I learned SQL, PHP and in highschool and loved it. I had quite a few jobs along my journey that had a certain data portion with them. In a few cases, I searched for data to analyse if none was included in the actual job description. The data insights I generated helped the store and manager I was working for.

Data in Today's World

Data is everywhere and in my opinion the next technological revolution will be around data. AI is certainly leading the charge, even though it is still in it's infancy. We data Analysts might be foot soldiers, but that is where the magic happens. From problem statement to finished presentation with recommendations, we are there!
